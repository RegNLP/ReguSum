SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION

17 CFR Part 230


General Rules and Regulations, Securities Act of 1933

CFR Correction

    This rule is being published by the Office of the Federal Register 
to correct an editorial or technical error that appeared in the most 
recent annual revision of the Code of Federal Regulations.
    In Title 17 of the Code of Federal Regulations, Parts 200 to 239, 
revised as of April 1, 2022, make the following corrections:


Sec.  230.482  [Corrected]

0
1. Amend Sec.  230.482 in paragraph (a) by removing the note with the 
heading ``Note to Paragraph (a)''.


Sec.  230.498  [Corrected]

0
2. Amend Sec.  230.498 in paragraph (f)(2) by removing the phrase ``a 
Notice under Sec.  270.30e-3 of this chapter,'' after ``Summary 
Prospectus'', and adding ``a Notice under Sec.  270.30e-3 of this 
chapter,'' after the phrase ``Statutory Prospectuses,''.
